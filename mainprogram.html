Welcome To Royal Woods...

Royal Woods is just the right mix of peaceful relaxation and buzzing activity, which goes into making a fabulous holiday for one and all.

Royal Woods, a unit of The Royal Hospitalities, another venture after The Pirache Art Hotel, is one of the finest getaways in North Goa, just 32kms away from the Dabolim International Airport and 22 kms from the railway station. It is centrally located between the famous Calangute and Candolim beaches, in holiday street ,surrounded by cafés, pubs, shopping arcades, churches, and hospital. Although situated in a highly urbanized area, guests will enjoy the laid back and serene environment the city has to offer.

Royal Woods is an eco-friendly hotel, just 100 metres before the Calangute beach with only 12 private wooden cottages, skilfully designed to offer a fresh, new take on four star luxury hotels in Goa. Every room is accentuated with warm earthly colours and wooden texture, thus creating a relaxing and cozy stay.

A Welcome drink (non-alcoholic) on arrival, a fruit basket, mineral water bottle complimentary, fully equipped mini-bar, a multi-cuisine restaurant and much more, all served with a true spirit of Goan hospitality.

Room Types

Every room at Royal Woods is designed in a way, only to enhance the relaxing atmosphere and a sense of space.

Royal Premium Room:-

Our Royal Premium rooms are tastefully decorated to give you a sense of comfort with all the essentials you require. A king size bed, 40”inch LED TV, free Wi-Fi, glass console shower, bathrobe, slippers, mini bar (on chargeable basis), iron and tea/coffee maker with amenities, all available to enrich your stay.

1. Can accommodate up to 2 adults
2. Children below 5 years stay free*
3. Late check-out on Sunday at 3:00 pm "Offer subject to availability"
4. Pets not allowed




Royal Imperium Room:-

Our Royal Imperium rooms are spacious and exude an ambiance of tranquillity. Adding to your comfort is a king size bed, 40”inch LED TV, free Wi-Fi, glass console shower, bathrobe, slippers, mini bar (on chargeable basis), iron and tea/coffee maker with amenities. One can also relax and experience the fresh air from the attached balcony.

1. Can accommodate up to 2 adults

Dining In Comfort:

"Royal Resto", our multi-cuisine restaurant, beside the swimming pool, is a perfect place to enjoy a sumptuous meal or a drink, any time from morning to midnight. Hearty breakfast sets, authentic Mediterranean and Continental food, light snacks, desserts are all available in the a la carte menu. The wide list of non-alcoholic and alcoholic beverages will not disappoint you either.
